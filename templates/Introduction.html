Project Title : Personal Finance Tracker

Overview:

In today's complex financial landscape, managing personal finances effectively is essential for financial stability and growth. The Personal Finance Tracker is a comprehensive database management system (DBMS) project aimed at helping users monitor and control their financial activities. This system offers a structured approach to tracking income, expenses, savings, and investments, enabling users to gain better control over their finances.

Objective:

The objective of the Personal Finance Tracker is to empower users by providing a tool that simplifies financial management. Through this system, users can track their financial transactions, categorize their spending, set budgets, and generate insightful reports. The system is designed to help users make informed decisions, ultimately leading to better financial planning.
